Higher Education System in Poland

  • Diverse Universities and Educational Institutions:

    • Poland has over 400 higher education institutions, including public and private universities, offering a wide range of programs from science and technology to medicine and arts. Notable institutions include the University of Warsaw and Jagiellonian University in Kraków

  • International Recognition:

    • Poland is a member of the Bologna Process, ensuring that its degrees are recognized across Europe and beyond. This also facilitates academic mobility and career opportunities for graduates worldwide.

  • Research and Innovation:

    • Poland invests significantly in research and innovation, offering excellent opportunities for students interested in research and development.


Why studying in Poland?

Poland provides a high-quality education system that is globally recognized, with tuition fees significantly lower than in other European countries such as Germany or the UK. Additionally, the cost of living in Poland is very affordable, making it an ideal destination for students seeking excellent education without excessive expenses.

scholarships

No matter which major you study at a foreign university, finance will always be an important factor. It’s highly recommended that you understand the costs of studying abroad, which generally include tuition fees, accommodation, travel, living expenses and so on. Now there are 5 kinds of ISSP scholarships available to international students, which are:

Erasmus+ program.


A European Union program that supports student mobility within and outside Europe, Offers scholarships for Moroccan students to study in Poland for master's programs or student exchange, Covers tuition fees, accommodation, and travel expenses.

Polish Government Scholarships (NAWA Scholarships)


Available for international students, including Moroccans, for bachelor’s, master’s, or PhD studies. Covers tuition fees, a monthly stipend, and accommodation support. Applications can be submitted through the official NAWA portal

Islamic Development Bank (IsDB) Scholarships.


Open to students from member countries of the Organization of Islamic Cooperation, including Morocco. Covers tuition fees, living expenses, health insurance, and a monthly stipend. Can be used for studies in accredited Polish universities.

Polish Embassy Scholarships.


Often linked to cultural and educational cooperation programs between Poland and Morocco.
