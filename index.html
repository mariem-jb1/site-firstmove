About Us

Our company was founded in 2010, by a group of Moroccan students who completed their studies abroad. the idea came from thier own experience as they initially faced significant challenges in finding the right guidance and support and even knowing about available scholarships.

This experience led to the creation of our company "First move", with the aim of helping students in their journey to study abroad by providing them with all the necessary information and guidance to achieve their academic goals.

Since our inception, we have successfully guided and sent over 5,000 students to universities and educational institutions around the world. We are proud to be part of their success stories.

We are committed to delivering the highest quality services and ensuring personalized guidance for each student based on their unique needs. Our vision is to be the trusted partner for every student striving to achieve their academic ambitions.
